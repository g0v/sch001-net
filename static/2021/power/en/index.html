Sch001 2021-22 Final

EN | 中

"Sch001" project is a g0v project that focuses on education. It puts 0 and 1 representing the digital native generation in the "School" to invite everyone to rethink the meaning of school. Sch001 also represents the connection and collaboration between education and the digital community.

Therefore, Sch001 invited contributors in the g0v community to transfer their experience of the Civic Tech project into courses and brought to students, educators, and newbies in the g0v community. Sch001 keeps being the bridge to connect schools and the g0v community to break the framework of school so that everyone can spontaneously collaborate across areas and ages and then change society through action.

In 2021, Sch001 held two local hackathons, seven virtual/offline camps, and nine workshops/lectures. We received 10 Edtech proposals and selected six winning teams on Demo Day, including "Citizen science for data-driven astronomy education," "Sync Slide," "CourseAPI," "Taiwan K-12 Education Resource Integration Platform," "Strange Pinglin," and "Swipe for study buddies --PinTwo."

Swipe for study buddies --PinTwo

This project will mainly focus on matching people by a discord bot as UI to help users find study buddies faster without putting a lot of efforts doing research but not getting what you need.
Basically, it will be represented by the user interface similar to dating app like Tinder, we only require users to choose tags which represent their interests, and what you would like to learn or your professions, then the algorithm will filter out some people who have the interests similar to you then you can choose whether you feel like to have deep convo with them. Furthermore, we have a rating and report system to help making the matching process smoother.

零時小學校給你靠 高中生寫「交學伴」軟體 線上學習不孤單
寫程式可以改變社會？由愛好資訊科技的「宅宅」組成的「g0v零時政府」，前年開辦「g0v零時小學校」（Sch001），推廣教學用的社群協作工具、開放資料，促進線上自學與共學。在台灣疫情升溫、實施遠距教學的期間，零時小學校是全台師生最有力的後盾。
