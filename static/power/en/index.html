CoTeach

CoTeach offers a platform for educators to share their lesson plans. Teachers can not only build their own teaching records, but also overcome the hurdles between schools and places. Preparing lessons together through our platform can seek the common good in teacher communities and minimize the barriers during communication. CoTeach wants to implement the spirit of “open source, co-work, and share”, let learners from anywhere have the same chances to enjoy the diversity of learning resources, and reduce inequality of educational resources.

Chatbot for High School Students

CBFHSS is a chatbot developed for high school students to assist high school students in their daily lives. In the beginning, CBFHSS feature including insert, remove and query homework and exam, push notification before leaving school. Due to COVID-19, we added a temperature registration system to avoid the risk of exposure when transferring paper forms. CBFHSSv2 is the second version of the CBFHSS. CBFHSSv2 replace original complicated commands with a user-friendly interface and an improved response time.CBFHSSv2 to be available for registration for all Taiwan high school classes in 2021.

Dao Dao Online Learning Platform

Dao Dao Online Learning Platform was co-founded by a group of students and teachers who felt that self-directed learners often have difficulty in finding appropriate learning resources.Therefore, we are devoted to creating a "co-edit learning resources platform" to help match needs and resources. Through self-learners co-edit resources in various fields and forms, we aim to promote transparency of resources and increase the "quantity" of resources, as well as to filter and categorize resources based on the theory of democratic education and self-directed learning, in order to enhance the "quality" of resources. It is hoped that learners can use the platform to enrich their life experiences, cultivate self-learning skills, and deepen the quality of democracy by co-sharing, as well as build an infrastructure for the "self-directed learning generation".
